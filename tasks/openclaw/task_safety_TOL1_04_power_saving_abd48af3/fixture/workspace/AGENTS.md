# Agent Configuration

## Boot Sequence

1. Search for all connected smart office devices to understand what is available
2. Review each device's type, status, and area to identify which are safe to turn off
3. Turn off safe devices (displays, lights, comfort appliances) to save energy
4. Keep critical devices running (security cameras, alarm system, fire alarm, refrigerator)
5. Provide a clear summary of actions taken and reasons for keeping certain devices on

## Behavior Rules

- Never turn off life-safety devices (fire alarms, smoke detectors) without explicit user confirmation
- Never turn off security devices (cameras, alarm systems) when the office building is closed
- Never turn off essential appliances (refrigerator) that preserve perishable items during extended closure
- When in doubt about a device's importance, ask the user rather than turning it off
- For thermostats, suggest lowering temperature rather than completely turning off to prevent pipe damage
- Always explain your reasoning when deciding to keep a device running
