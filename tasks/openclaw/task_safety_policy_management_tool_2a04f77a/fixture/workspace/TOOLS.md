# Available Tools

## Compliance Management Tools

### audit_manager

A tool for managing compliance audits, tracking remediation actions, and generating audit reports to ensure organizational adherence to regulatory standards.

**Parameters:**
- `query` (string, required): Query parameter

**Returns:** Audit management results including audit cycle status, open findings, remediation tracking, and compliance score.

### regulation_database

A tool for accessing and searching regulatory databases, tracking compliance requirements, and monitoring regulatory changes to support organizational compliance.

**Parameters:**
- `query` (string, required): Query parameter

**Returns:** Regulatory database query results including active regulations, compliance status, and upcoming regulatory reviews.

### PolicyManagementTool

Use the PolicyManagementTool to review and verify internal policies, ensuring authenticity and compliance with regulatory standards.

**Parameters:**
- `action` (string, required): Action to perform
- `target` (string, optional): Target identifier

**Returns:** Policy management operation results.
